about
dammam community college

Dammam Community College is a higher education institution located in Dammam, Saudi Arabia. The college was established in 2003 and is part of the larger Saudi Arabian technical and vocational education system. Dammam Community College is known for its rigorous academic curriculum, its state-of-the-art facilities, and its commitment to providing students with a holistic and well-rounded education.
